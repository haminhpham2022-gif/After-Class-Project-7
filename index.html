Music

What is Music?

Music, both a form of art and entertainment, is the product of assembling rhythms, vocals, melody and harmony to create a sound, all while expressing emotions through it.

Genres of Music

There are plently of diffrent genres in music, with these being some of the most common ones.

  • Pop
  • Rap
  • Hiphop
  • RnB

Does music have a purpose?

Nowadays people use music as a way to express their feelings, some things can be put into words easier while some simply can't be said. Music gives people the chance to share a part about themselves without having to put it into speech and rather having it be understood through sounds.
